I see programming as one of our most complex intellectual activities. We are attempting to build a flawless logical edifice with the finite, fallible tool of the human mind. The number of possible states and execution paths in any significant program is astronomically large, exceeding our capacity for perfect foresight.

This process is like trying to write a complete legal code for a society from scratch. You can apply immense rigor and logic, but you cannot possibly anticipate every future scenario or human interaction.
